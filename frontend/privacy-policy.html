Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to JobPulse ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our job application tracking service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password (encrypted) when you sign up
  • Google Account Data: Email address, name, and profile picture when you sign in with Google
  • Gmail Data: When you connect your Gmail account, we access job-related emails to extract:
    • Company names
    • Job titles
    • Application statuses
    • Application dates
    • Email content related to job applications
  • Job Application Data: Information you manually enter or that we extract from your emails
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our service (features used, login times)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our job tracking service
  • Scan your Gmail for job application emails (only when you explicitly authorize)
  • Parse and organize job application information
  • Display your job application statistics and tracking data
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Send you important service updates (optional)
  • Improve our service and develop new features

4. How We Store Your Information

We take data security seriously:

  • Encryption: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. Gmail app passwords and OAuth tokens are encrypted using Fernet encryption
  • Database: Data is stored securely in MongoDB Atlas with authentication and encryption at rest
  • Access Control: Only authenticated users can access their own data via JWT tokens
  • Retention: We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time

5. Gmail Access and Permissions

When you connect your Gmail account:

  • We request read-only access to your Gmail (gmail.readonly scope)
  • We ONLY scan emails from known job platforms and recruiters
  • We NEVER read, store, or access personal emails unrelated to job applications
  • We DO NOT send emails on your behalf
  • We DO NOT share your Gmail data with third parties
  • You can revoke Gmail access at any time through your Google Account settings or our app

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Google OAuth: We use Google's authentication services to verify your identity and access Gmail (with your permission)
  • MongoDB Atlas: Our database provider, which follows industry-standard security practices
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we have stored about you
  • Correct: Update or correct your information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Revoke: Disconnect your Gmail account at any time
  • Object: Object to processing of your data

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and local storage for:

  • Authentication tokens (JWT) to keep you logged in
  • User preferences and settings
  • We do NOT use third-party advertising or tracking cookies

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Password hashing with bcrypt
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive credentials
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication

10. Children's Privacy

JobPulse is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

12. International Users

JobPulse is hosted and operated in accordance with applicable data protection laws. By using our service, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data as described in this policy.
